r_key: replay the last move.

n_key: restart the last game from the begening.

f_key: enter & exit full screen mode.

e_key: exit & return to title screen.

up & down arrows_keys: change difficulty.

left and right arrows_keys: change pawn type.

move pawns with the mouse by clicking on them.

jump is mandatory,jump by clicking on the jumping pawn.

if you have several  jump for one pawn:

select where to jump with the mouse then click on the pawn to jump.
